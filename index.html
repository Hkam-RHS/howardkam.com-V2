STEM CAPSTONE 1 and 2

Two years:

Capstone 1. Cybersecurity focus

Students that are taking STEM Capstone for the first time will learn about Cybersecurity strategies, write code that finds threats, and perform mock cyber attacks.

Capstone 2. STEM Capstone project

A year long self-directed project in which the student will research and present a project to a panel of judges. For Seniors that are currently earning, or have earned, 4 credits of Math and Science with a 3.0 GPA, this is an opportunity to earn STEM Capstone honors for graduation.

Students that are in the capstone project must meet with Mr. Kam during the first two weeks of the course to identify and submit their proposal.
